About IronForge

NOT JUST A GYM.
A FORGE.

Founded in 2019 by competitive powerlifter Marcus Steel, IronForge was built with one mission: create a space where ordinary people achieve extraordinary results. We combine cutting-edge equipment with world-class coaching in an atmosphere that demands excellence.

Our 15,000 sq ft facility features state-of-the-art strength platforms, an Olympic lifting area, cardio zones, a recovery suite, and dedicated group training studios. Everything you need. Nothing you don't.
